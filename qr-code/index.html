About This QR Code Generator

Generate QR codes for URLs, plain text, email addresses, phone numbers, WiFi credentials, and SMS messages. All processing happens in your browser — no data is sent to any server.

Supported Types

  • URL — Link to any website. Scan to open in browser.
  • Text — Encode any text message.
  • Email — Opens email compose with prefilled address and subject.
  • Phone — Scan to dial a phone number.
  • WiFi — Share WiFi credentials. Scan to auto-connect (Android/iOS).
  • SMS — Opens SMS with prefilled number and message.
