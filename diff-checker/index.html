About This Diff Checker

Compare two blocks of text and see exactly what changed. Line-by-line comparison with color-coded additions, deletions, and modifications — similar to git diff but in your browser.

Features

  • Line-by-Line Diff — Clear visualization of every change
  • Character-Level Highlighting — See exactly which characters changed within modified lines
  • Options — Trim whitespace, ignore case for flexible comparison
  • Statistics — Count of additions, deletions, modifications, and unchanged lines
  • Privacy — 100% client-side, no data leaves your browser
