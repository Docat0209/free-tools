About This Color Palette Generator

      
Create harmonious color palettes based on color theory. Pick a base color and choose a scheme — analogous, complementary, triadic, or monochromatic. Copy hex codes or CSS variables for your project.

      
Color Schemes Explained

      
    
        
  • Analogous — Colors adjacent on the color wheel. Harmonious and natural.
    • 
        
  • Complementary — Opposite colors. High contrast, vibrant.
    • 
        
  • Triadic — Three colors equally spaced. Balanced and colorful.
    • 
        
  • Split Complementary — Base + two colors adjacent to its complement.
    • 
        
  • Tetradic — Four colors forming a rectangle on the wheel.
    • 
        
  • Monochromatic — Variations of one hue. Clean and elegant.
    • 
        
  • Shades — Light to dark variations of the base color.
