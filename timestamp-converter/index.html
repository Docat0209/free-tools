About This Timestamp Converter

Convert between Unix timestamps (epoch time) and human-readable dates. A Unix timestamp represents the number of seconds since January 1, 1970 00:00:00 UTC (the Unix epoch).

Features

  • Live Clock — Real-time current Unix timestamp
  • Bidirectional — Convert timestamp → date and date → timestamp
  • Milliseconds — Support for both seconds and milliseconds
  • Multiple Formats — UTC, ISO 8601, local time, relative time
  • Common Timestamps — Quick reference for relative times
  • Privacy — 100% client-side, no data leaves your browser
