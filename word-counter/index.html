About This Word Counter

This free word counter instantly analyzes your text as you type. Count words, characters (with and without spaces), sentences, and paragraphs. Get reading and speaking time estimates based on average speeds of 200 and 130 words per minute respectively.

Features

  • Real-time counting — Stats update instantly as you type
  • Keyword analysis — See your top 10 most-used words (excluding common stop words)
  • Reading time — Based on 200 words per minute average reading speed
  • Speaking time — Based on 130 words per minute average speaking speed
  • Copy stats — Export all stats to clipboard in one click
  • Privacy — 100% client-side, your text never leaves your browser
